Using both Java and XML in the Android studio IDE, the Gainz R Us app is brought to life. Java and XML together allow the mesh between creativity and engagement. Our app was made to be straightforward and intuitive, with no distractions, you can get right to work.

No workouts in mind?

Quickly choose to generate a random workout by type or intensity and get right to work. No plan, no stress, Gainz R Us has your back!

Log and view workout history

Seamlessly save workouts by logging them. Options include exercise, sets, reps, weight, and workout rating. Past workouts are just a finger-tap away.

Shatter records

With Gainz R Us, the sky's the limit. Every new weightlifting record for the big 3 compound exercises are tracked and displayed, so you know your next big milestone!

Embark on a new journey

Swiftly erase all data you've ever entered. Unlock a new chapter of exercise records.
