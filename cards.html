Hearts

Interactive C++ game

Hearts is a simple card game played by 4 people, in this case, 1 user and 3 computers. The object of the game is to play cards strategically in a round-by-round format to accumulate the least amount of total points at the end.

Ready...

Hearts is a fun card game you can play at your own leisure against 3 computers. It's easily accessible on any Windows computer terminal or through a C++ compatible IDE. Check the GitHub repo for more info.

Steady...

The goal of the game is to finish with the least amount of points. You can only see your cards and you won't know what each of the computers play until you play a card. Each player's cards are sorted by suit (Diamond, Heart, Spade, Club) then by descending value (A, K, Q, J, 10...2).

Hint

Remember cards that have/haven't been played. Also note that computers will play the top most card with a matching suit or the top most card of their decks each round.

Duel!

The game will start with whoever has the 2 of Clubs. Then, the person who plays the highest value card of the leading suit each round will be first to play the next round. That person who plays the highest value card of the leading suit will also accumulate the point total of that round. After 13 rounds there will be 1 winner, whomever accumulated the least amount of points. Goodluck!
