Income inequality in Brazil

Our website addresses income inequality in Brazil and consists of engaging visual aids, such as images, carousels, and different animations. The website aims to educate our audience about the extent of the income gap in Brazil and the impact it has on different groups. Check it out here.

Issues in Brazil

We analyzed the factors contributing to Brazil's income inequality, including environmental, social, and economic factors and their impact on different groups, including women, minorities, and the rural poor.

Goals

Each of our goals pages consist of the same layout with different context. The layouts boast a jumbotron with an image to match the theme of the goal, a quick 3 fact carousel, and an option to reveal a card-deck container with more information on what Brazil is doing to reach these goals and succeed as a country.
The pages from in order: Quality of Life, Economic conditions, and Sustainability and Climate control.
